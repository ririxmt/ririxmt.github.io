Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use data from your TikTok account to provide our analytics services.

1. Data We Collect

We only access data that you explicitly authorize through the TikTok Login Kit. This includes:

  • Public Profile Information (user.info.profile): Displaying your bio and profile verification status.
  • Account Statistics (user.info.stats): Monitoring follower counts and overall engagement trends.
  • Video Metadata (video.list): Analyzing performance metrics such as views, likes, shares, and comments for public videos.

2. How We Use Your Data

The collected data is used exclusively to generate automated performance reports and interactive analytics dashboards. This allows you to monitor your content strategy effectively. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Data Security and Retention

We only store the necessary access tokens required to maintain your data connection. We do not use your data for unauthorized tracking or training of machine learning models. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

4. User Control and Revocation

You have full control over your data. You can revoke our access at any time through your TikTok App Security Settings. Once access is revoked, we will immediately stop fetching any further data from your account.
